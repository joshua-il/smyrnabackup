On March 17th 2005,Nations Reach team went to visit Sri Lanka. Our mission partners from Nuewralia were waiting for us in Colombo. Our plan was to visit the worst hit Tsunami areas in Sri Lanka. The purpose of this trip was to identify the need for workers and to prepare project proposals for Nations Reach to consider future developments.

March17, 2005

On March 17, 2005 I left India on Jet Airways at 12 p.m. and headed towards Sri Lanka.

After arriving in Sri Lanka, we traveled by car to go to Kalmunai via Kandy. I met with Pastor Selvarajah at 11.30 p.m after a 9 hour drive from the airport.

Had a meeting with all the local workers and went to view several Tsunami disaster areas;
  • Visited the most effected areas in Sri Lanka.
  • Went through army restricted areas to see how the camps have been setup.
  • Visited camps controlled by the TASK FORCE.
  • Visited camps controlled by LTTE.
  • Had a meeting with local Little Roses staffs, and potential future workers for Little Roses.
  • Met with Kannan who is in need of support for his work, and a church building that was completely destroyed by the Tsunami.
  • Had a discussion with Selvarajah for our future leadership conference and partnership in missions.
  • Handed over check for Tsunami relief work.
